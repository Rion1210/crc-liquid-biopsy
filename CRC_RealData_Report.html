Part A — Microbiome classifier

Data

649 stool samples drawn from 7 published CRC cohorts (ZellerG_2014, FengQ_2015, YuJ_2015, VogtmannE_2016, HanniganGD_2017, ThomasAM_2018a, ThomasAM_2018b) via the curatedMetagenomicData ExperimentHub.

Model

RandomForest (500 trees, min_samples_leaf=5, balanced class weight) on log10-transformed relative abundances. 10-fold stratified cross-validation; every sample predicted by a model trained on the other 9 folds.
